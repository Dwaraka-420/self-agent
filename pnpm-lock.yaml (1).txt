# pnpm-lock.yaml
This file locks the dependencies and their versions for the project, ensuring consistent installations across environments.

- `lockfileVersion`: Indicates the version of the pnpm lockfile format.
- `dependencies`: Lists the direct dependencies and their versions.
- `devDependencies`: Lists development dependencies and their versions.
- `packages`: Includes metadata about all resolved dependencies, their integrity checks, and peer dependencies.
Used by the `pnpm` package manager to guarantee reproducible builds.
